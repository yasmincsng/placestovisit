Why Spain is Special to Me

Spain captivates me with its passionate culture, stunning architecture, and warm, welcoming people. The country's ability to preserve its rich history while embracing modern life is truly inspiring. From flamenco dancing in Andalusia to the avant-garde designs of Barcelona, Spain offers an incredible diversity of experiences.

The Spanish lifestyle of enjoying long meals with family and friends, the siesta culture, and the late-night tapas bars all reflect a philosophy of life that values joy and connection. The country's regional diversity means every visit can offer something completely new.

How to Get There from the Philippines

Traveling from the Philippines to Spain requires some planning but is definitely worthwhile:

  • By Air: There are no direct flights. Common routes include Manila to Madrid via Middle Eastern hubs (Dubai, Doha) or European cities, with total travel time around 18-24 hours.
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring (April-June) and Fall (September-October) offer pleasant temperatures. Summer can be very hot, especially in southern Spain.
  • Visa Requirements: Philippine passport holders need a Schengen visa to visit Spain.

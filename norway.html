Why Norway is Special to Me

Norway captivates me with its raw natural beauty and peaceful way of life. The dramatic fjords carved by glaciers, the dancing northern lights, and the midnight sun create landscapes that seem almost otherworldly. There's something magical about the Norwegian concept of "friluftsliv" (open-air living) that deeply resonates with my love for nature.

The country's commitment to sustainability and quality of life is inspiring. From the cozy wooden cabins to the modern Scandinavian architecture, Norway offers a perfect balance between tradition and innovation. The idea of witnessing the aurora borealis from a glass igloo or hiking through untouched wilderness makes Norway one of my top dream destinations.

How to Get There from the Philippines

Traveling from the Philippines to Norway requires some planning but is absolutely worth it:

  • By Air: No direct flights. Common routes include Manila to Oslo via Singapore, Dubai, or Doha (total travel time 16-20 hours). Major airlines include Singapore Airlines, Emirates, and Qatar Airways.
  • Best Time to Visit: Summer (June-August) for midnight sun and hiking, or Winter (December-February) for northern lights and winter sports.
  • Visa Requirements: Philippine passport holders need a Schengen visa to visit Norway. Apply at the Norwegian embassy or VFS Global.
  • Currency: Norwegian Krone (NOK). Credit cards widely accepted.

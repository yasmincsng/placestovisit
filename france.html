Why France is Special to Me

France embodies the art de vivre (art of living) that I deeply admire. From the romantic streets of Paris to the sun-drenched vineyards of Bordeaux, every region offers its own unique charm. The French appreciation for beauty - in their food, architecture, fashion, and even everyday life - is something that speaks to my soul.

What makes France truly magical to me is how it celebrates both grandeur and simplicity. You can marvel at the Palace of Versailles in the morning and enjoy a perfect baguette with cheese by the Seine in the afternoon. The language, the café culture, and the way every meal is an occasion - these are the things that make France one of my dream destinations.

How to Get There from the Philippines

Traveling from the Philippines to France is straightforward with these options:

  • By Air: Direct flights from Manila to Paris (Charles de Gaulle) take about 14-15 hours. Airlines include Philippine Airlines, Air France, and Qatar Airways.
  • Best Time to Visit: Spring (April-June) or Fall (September-October) for pleasant weather and fewer crowds.
  • Visa Requirements: Philippine passport holders need a Schengen visa to visit France.
  • Currency: Euro (€). Credit cards widely accepted.

Authentic French Experiences

  • Market Day: Shop like a local at a traditional French market
  • Wine Tasting: Sample world-class wines in Bordeaux or Burgundy
  • Patisserie Tour: Discover the art of French pastry-making
  • Bike Ride: Cycle through vineyards or along the Loire River
  • Cooking Class: Learn to make classic French dishes
